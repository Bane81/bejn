Lynda myers lost 80lbs and 4 sizes


In 2004, I was 18 years old and tragically lost my father. I began living an unhealthy lifestyle which left me weighing 215lbs. Almost 10 years later, in December 2013, when so many New Year’s Resolutions were being made, I decided to finally lose my excess weight. This decision came after breaking down crying at my doctor’s office. I hated the way I looked and the way I felt being overweight. I explained to him how, “I was working so hard running and trying to lose weight…but it wasn’t working.” His response was, “Well, Rebecca…it’s great you’re running, but it really doesn’t matter if you’re not eating healthy!”
